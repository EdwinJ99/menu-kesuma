About Us

ETHSEM (Ethnopedagogy-STEM for Indonesia Education) is an initiative that combines local wisdom with the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) approach to create a more contextual, innovative, and relevant education system.
We adhere to the educational philosophy of Ki Hadjar Dewantara, namely Kodrat Alam ("The Nature of Nature") and Kodrat Zaman ("The Nature of Times").

  • Teacher Training and Competency Enhancement
  • Creation of Digital and Interactive Learning Materials
  • Research and Development of Learning Models
